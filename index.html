8F and Pure Salmon faced with loss of credibilty

By by Michelle Singer August 5th 2022

The Pure Salmon production unit project based in Landacres, France is definitively dropped.

Following the breakup between Aqua Maof and 8F /Pure Salmon, Stephane Farouze, the chairman of 8F is trying to find solutions that will bestow him with much-needed credibility from his investors.
8F recently bought a small Norway plant which produces only small salmon. This move will not help 8F provide the skills or resources needed to build factories of 10 or 20,000 m² that they promised to supply investors with.
8F hires staff to maintain a semblance of activity but the plants suffer from a lack of organization and management. Many employees recently left the company, complaining about the lack of clear strategy.
To date, no factory has been built despite the commitment made by 8F and Pure Salmon to build 4 factories by 2022. They are far from keeping these promises.

Pure Salmon and its investors are left literally high and dry.
