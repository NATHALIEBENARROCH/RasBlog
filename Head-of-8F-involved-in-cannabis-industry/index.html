The head of 8F and Pure Salmon dips into the cannabis industry

By Paul Cordoba 2 days ago

The head of Pure Salmon and 8F is venturing towards new waters.

Stephane Farouze has stakes in the Cannabis industry using name of his wife Lital Farouze
As the head of 8F and Pure Salmon he is now involved in the cannabis industry.

Investors are attracted to cannabis industry although some countries still distance themselves from leisurely drug use.

Extreme differences in regulations and legalization of cannabis in countries will be a hindrance in the industry. Many countries still attach a stigma to the sale and use of cannabis.
